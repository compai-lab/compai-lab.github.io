Four abstracts accepted at 2025 ISMRM & ISMRT Annual Meeting

Hannah Eichhorn, Natascha Niessen, Ha Young Kim
Feb 3, 2025 1 min read

Four abstracts from our group have been accepted at the 2025 ISMRM & ISMRT Annual Meeting.

  • Hannah Eichhorn will present her work “Leveraging Acquisition Knowledge to Enhance Robustness of Physics-Informed Motion Correction for T2* Quantification” as an oral power pitch on Monday, 12 May 2025 at 4 pm HAST.

  • Hannah Eichhorn will additionally present her work “Assessing the Influence of Preprocessing on the Agreement of Image Quality Metrics with Radiological Evaluation in the Presence of Motion” as an oral power pitch on Monday, 12 May 2025 at 4 pm HAST. Hannah’s abstract received a MAGNA CUM LAUDE Merit Award.

  • Natascha Niessen will present her work “Probing the sparsity of the MPnRAGE sequence through subspace compression” as a digital poster on Thursday, 15 May 2025 at 2:15 pm HAST.

  • Ha Young Kim will present her work “Deep learning enabled fast free-breathing stack-of-stars multiparameter mapping for fully quantitative analysis of prostate carcinoma” as a digital poster on Monday, 12 May 2025 at 5 pm HAST.

Hannah Eichhorn
Doctoral Researcher

Hannah Eichhorn’s research focuses on deep learning-based reconstruction and motion correction of multi-parametric brain MRI.

Natascha Niessen
Doctoral Researcher

My research interests include Deep Learning-enabled MRI for application in Alzheimer’s Disease.

Ha Young Kim
Doctoral Researcher

My research interests include machine learning for MRI reconstruction and postprocessing.
