Zeyad Mahmoud presenting at Helmholtz Imaging Conference 2025
Zeyad Mahmoud’s abstract has been selected for an oral presentation at the Helmholtz Imaging Conference 2025. Zeyad presented his Master’s thesis research, titled “Unsupervised Temporal Diffusion-Based Interpolation for 4D CT”. In this study, he investigated the ability of deep generative models, specifically diffusion models, for interpolating temporal 4D chest CT scans.
