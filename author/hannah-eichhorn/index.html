Hannah Eichhorn

PhD student

Technical University Munich

Helmholtz Center Munich

Hannah Eichhorn is a PhD student at the Institute of Machine Learning in Biomedical Imaging (IML), Helmholtz Munich. Her research focuses on deep-learning based reconstruction of multi-parametric magnetic resonance brain imaging. Her PhD project is a collaboration between IML and the Neuroscientific MR-Physics research group at Klinikum rechts der Isar.

Interests
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging
  • Deep learning
  • Image reconstruction
